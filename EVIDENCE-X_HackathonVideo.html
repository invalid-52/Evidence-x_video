THE PROBLEM
Why Digitization Alone Fails
India generates 50,000+ handwritten FIRs daily. Every single one is legally fragile.
01
Data Inaccessibility
Handwritten Telugu & English FIRs trapped in physical archives. State-wide pattern detection and cross-district intelligence is impossible.
02
Legal Fragility
AI transcriptions without a provable link to the original are routinely challenged in court. Hallucinations create inadmissible records.
03
Integrity Risk
No granular audit trail means records are vulnerable to unauthorized modification, deletion, or going 'missing'. No accountability exists.
📊  85% of FIRs remain handwritten  ·  OCR-only solutions fail all three pillars: Accuracy, Accountability, Admissibility

Validation UI

YELLOW 60–90% — Review recommended. Click to inspect.
RED <60% — MANDATORY. Auto-focus. Must approve.
